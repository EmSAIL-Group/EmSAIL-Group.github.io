About

EmSAIL Lab focuses on next-generation embodied spatial intelligence systems for robotics operating in complex real-world environments. Our goal is to develop intelligent robotic systems that integrate spatial perception, representation, reasoning, and action into a unified embodied intelligence pipeline.

Research Directions

  • Spatial Perception and SLAM
  • 3D / 4D Scene Representation
  • Gaussian Splatting for Robotics
  • Vision-Language-Action Models
  • Robot Policy Learning
  • Embodied World Models
